Tourist Attractions

There are a multitude of options for any visitors! From the iconic Gardens by the Bay to the Signapore Zoo! Here are our recommendations:

  • Gardens by the Bay — beautiful park with stunning architecture
  • Universal Studios Singapore — amusement park offering unique rides and experiences
  • Signapore Zoo — visit the beautiful zoo with diverse animals
  • Merlion Park — home of the iconic Merlion statue which has rich symbolism— commemorating Singapore's fish village origins

Good Eats

Savor both local and global cuisines

Singapore is a food lover's paradise, with options from hawker stalls to restaurants. Here are our picks:

  • Maxwell Food Centre — very popular for Hainanese chicken rice and many other dishes
  • Chinatown Complex — huge variety of budget-friendly local eats
  • Lau Pa Sat — historic food market known for satay, a marinated grilled meat with a peanut-based dipping sauce
  • Jumbo Seafood — if you enjoy seafood of course, it's famous for chili and black pepper crab

Cultural Activities

Dive into Singaporean culture

Cultural Activities

Explore Singapore's rich cultural mix through these unique experiences:

  • National Gallery — wonderful gallery that showcases Southeast Asian art in many forms
  • Little India — a colorful area with temples for beautiful views and spice shops
  • Chinatown Heritage Centre — offering a glimpse into early migrant life and its rich history
  • Kampong Glam — a Malay heritage district with mosques, markets, and more

Transportation

Save money and go public

Transportation

Getting around is easy with Singapore's efficient transport system:

  • MRT — Mass Rapid Transport System, clean and reliable train network
  • EZ-Link Card — tap-and-go card for trains and buses
  • Tourist Pass — offers unlimited rides for a fixed price durring your stay
  • Public Buses — offers afforadable air-conditioned buses with wide coverage
